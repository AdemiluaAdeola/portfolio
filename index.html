Kobo
AI-Powered Personal Finance · Nigerian Market
JWT AuthSMS ParsingAI Coach Cash Flow ForecastSavings Spaces Debt IntelligenceOpen Banking Nudge EngineMoney Minute Rate LimitingDocker + Redis

AI personal finance for young Nigerian professionals

Kobo is a production-grade fintech backend built for the Nigerian financial context — where most transactions arrive as SMS alerts from GTBank, UBA, Zenith, Access Bank, First Bank, and Stanbic. Kobo parses these natively.

The system integrates esusu (cooperative savings) into its Spaces feature, understands black tax in financial coaching, and connects to Nigerian open banking providers via Mono and Okra webhooks with HMAC verification.

  • 14-day cash flow forecasting engine
  • AI financial coach (Anthropic API / OpenAI-compatible)
  • Nudge engine for automated financial recommendations
  • Debt Snowball & Avalanche payoff simulation
  • Savings Spaces with esusu-style group support
  • Shareable "Money Minute" financial health report
  • Integration test suite with async SQLAlchemy
